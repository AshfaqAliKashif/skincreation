Brightening Peel For Glass Skin

Face Neck

Achieving "glass skin" often involves using products that promote brightness, even skin tone, and a smooth complexion. A brightening peel can be a part of this skincare routine. However, it's important to note that chemical peels, even those designed for brightening, can be potent, and it's recommended to perform them cautiously and under professional guidance.

Here's a simple routine for a brightening peel aimed at achieving glass skin:

  • Cleanse
  • Pre-Peel Preparation
  • Brightening Peel
  • Post-Peel Soothing
  • Sun Protection
  • Frequency

Start with a once-a-week application and adjust based on your skin's tolerance. Overuse of peeling products can lead to irritation.
