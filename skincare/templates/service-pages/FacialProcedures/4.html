Luxury Hydra Facial

Rs. 12000

A luxury HydraFacial is an advanced skincare treatment that combines several steps to cleanse, exfoliate, extract impurities, hydrate, and protect the skin. The HydraFacial treatment is known for its effectiveness in improving skin texture, reducing fine lines and wrinkles, and promoting a more radiant complexion.

Here's a general overview of what you might expect during a luxury HydraFacial:

  • Cleansing and Exfoliation:
  • Acid Peel
  • Extraction
  • Hydration
  • Protection
  • LED Light Therapy (optional)

A luxury HydraFacial is typically performed by trained skincare professionals, and the specific products and techniques used may vary based on the spa or clinic offering the service. The treatment is generally well-tolerated and suitable for a variety of skin types. It's often recommended for those seeking immediate results with minimal downtime.
