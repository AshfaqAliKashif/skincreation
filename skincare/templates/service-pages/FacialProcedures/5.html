Carbon Facial / Black Doll Facial

Rs. 5000

"Carbon Facial" or "Black Doll Facial" generally refers to a specific type of laser treatment called the Q-switched Nd:YAG laser treatment with a carbon peel. It is also known by other names such as the "Carbon Laser Peel" or "Black Doll Laser Facial." This aesthetic procedure has gained popularity for its ability to address various skin concerns and provide a rejuvenated appearance.

Here's an overview of what the treatment typically involves:

  • Application of Carbon Paste
  • Laser Treatment
  • Skin Rejuvenation
  • Pore Cleansing
  • Skin Brightening
  • Minimal Downtime

It's essential to note that the effectiveness and safety of the Carbon Facial depend on various factors, including the individual's skin type, concerns, and the expertise of the practitioner performing the procedure. As with any cosmetic treatment, it's crucial to consult with a qualified skincare professional or dermatologist to determine if this type of facial is suitable for your skin and to ensure that you fully understand the potential risks and benefits.
