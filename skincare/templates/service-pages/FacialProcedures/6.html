Vampire Facial

Rs. 5000

The "Vampire Facial" is a cosmetic procedure that gained attention due to its association with celebrities and its unique approach to facial rejuvenation. The technical name for this procedure is Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy, and it involves the use of the patient's own blood to enhance the appearance of the skin.

Here's a brief overview of the VampireFacial process:

  • Blood Draw
  • Centrifugation
  • PRP Application
  • Activation of Platelets
  • Results

It's important to note that while the Vampire Facial has gained popularity, scientific evidence supporting its efficacy is somewhat limited, and individual results can vary. The procedure is generally considered safe when performed by a trained and qualified healthcare professional. However, as with any cosmetic procedure, it's crucial to have a consultation with a licensed practitioner to discuss your specific needs, expectations, and any potential risks.
