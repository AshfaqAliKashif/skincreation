Microblading

Rs. 10000

Microblading is a popular semi-permanent cosmetic tattooing technique used to enhance the appearance of eyebrows. It involves the manual application of pigment into the outer layers of the skin, creating hair-like strokes that mimic natural eyebrow hairs. This technique is commonly chosen by individuals who want to achieve fuller, more defined eyebrows or those with sparse or thinning brows.

Here are the key aspects of microblading:

  • Procedure
  • Pigments
  • Customization
  • Topical Anesthetic
  • Healing Process
  • Duration of Results

Before undergoing microblading, it's essential to research and choose a licensed and reputable professional who follows strict hygiene practices. Additionally, a thorough consultation with the technician is important to discuss expectations, desired outcomes, and any potential concerns or considerations.
