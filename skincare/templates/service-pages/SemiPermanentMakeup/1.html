BB Glow

Rs. 10000

BB Glow is a cosmetic treatment that gained popularity, particularly in South Korea, for its skin-brightening and complexion-enhancing effects. It involves the application of a tinted BB cream using microneedling or other techniques to provide a semi-permanent makeup effect. The procedure aims to improve skin tone, reduce imperfections, and create a radiant complexion.

Here are the key features of BB Glow:

  • BB Cream Application
  • Microneedling or Nano Needling
  • Even Skin Tone
  • Semi-Permanent Nature

While BB Glow has gained popularity for its immediate skin-brightening effects, it's important to note that the procedure's safety and long-term effects are still a subject of discussion within the skincare and aesthetics communities. Some concerns have been raised about the potential risks associated with the use of semi-permanent pigments and the need for thorough practitioner training.
