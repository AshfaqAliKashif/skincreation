Eyelash Extensions

Rs. 10000

Eyelash extensions are a popular cosmetic procedure that involves attaching individual synthetic or natural hair fibers to a person's natural eyelashes to enhance their length, thickness, and curl. The goal is to create a fuller and more dramatic look without the need for mascara or false eyelashes.

Here are the key aspects of eyelash extensions:

  • Material
  • Application Process
  • Variety of Styles
  • Maintenance
  • Duration
  • Potential Risks

It's crucial to research and choose a reputable salon or technician with proper training and hygiene practices when considering eyelash extensions. Additionally, individuals with sensitive eyes or allergies may want to do a patch test to ensure they don't have an adverse reaction to the adhesive used.
