Lip Tint

Rs. 10000

Lip tint, also known as lip stain or lip tint ink, is a cosmetic product designed to add color to the lips for a natural and long-lasting effect. Unlike traditional lipsticks, lip tints are formulated to provide a sheer and lightweight finish. They often come in liquid or gel form and are applied to the lips for a subtle and stained appearance.

Here are some key features of lip tints:

  • Formulation
  • Sheer Color
  • Long-Lasting
  • Buildable Coverage
  • Variety of Shades
  • Applicator

Lip tints have become popular for those who prefer a more natural and low-maintenance lip look. The subtle and long-lasting nature of lip tints makes them suitable for various occasions, from everyday wear to special events. As with any cosmetic product, individual preferences and skin types can influence the effectiveness and wear of lip tints.
