Hair PRP With Meso

Rs. 8000

PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy for hair is a non-surgical procedure that involves using the patient's own blood to stimulate hair growth. The procedure is based on the idea that the growth factors in platelets can help promote the regeneration of hair follicles, potentially leading to increased hair thickness and density.

Here's a brief overview of the PRP Hair process:

  • Blood Draw
  • Centrifugation
  • Antioxidant Protection
  • Injection

As with any medical procedure, it's crucial to consult with a qualified healthcare provider to determine if PRP therapy is a suitable option for your specific hair loss concerns. The effectiveness of PRP for hair may vary among individuals, and results are not guaranteed.
