HIFU

Rs. 15000

HIFU stands for High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound, and it is a non-invasive cosmetic procedure used for skin tightening and lifting. The technology utilizes focused ultrasound energy to penetrate deep into the skin tissues, stimulating collagen production and resulting in a firmer and lifted appearance.

Here's a brief overview of the HIFU process:

  • Ultrasound Waves
  • Focused Energy
  • Tissue Tightening
  • Collagen Stimulation

While HIFU is generally considered safe, it's crucial to have the procedure performed by a trained and experienced professional. If you're considering HIFU or any other cosmetic procedure, consult with a licensed practitioner to discuss your goals and assess the suitability of the treatment for your individual needs.
