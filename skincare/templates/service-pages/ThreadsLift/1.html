Thread Lift

Rs. 12000/Thread

A thread lift in aesthetics refers to a cosmetic procedure designed to lift and tighten sagging skin on the face or other parts of the body. This non-surgical technique involves the use of specialized threads that are inserted under the skin to lift and support the tissue. The threads used in this procedure can be absorbable or non-absorbable, and they may have barbs or cones to help anchor the skin in a lifted position.

As with any aesthetic procedure, it's crucial to choose a qualified and experienced practitioner to ensure safety and optimal results. Thread lifts may not be suitable for everyone, so a thorough consultation with a healthcare professional is essential to determine eligibility and discuss potential risks and benefits.
